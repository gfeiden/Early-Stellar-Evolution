Analytical Models

Stars undergoing quasi-hydrostatic contraction can be described by the canonical set of stellar structure equations at every point in time during their contraction. The stellar structure equations are
\(\frac{dP}{dr} = -\frac{G m(r) \rho(r)}{r^2} \textrm{\small (Hydrostatic Equilibrium)}\)
