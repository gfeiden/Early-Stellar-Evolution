Abstract

A review on the early evolution of stars. Commissioned by PA

Protostellar Evolution

Discussion of protostellar evolution, including a simple picture (gravitational collapse mediated by thermal pressure), evidence for/against the simple picture, and potential (re: likely) complications.

Basic Picture

In the simplest view, protostars are large balls of gas collapsing under the influence of self-gravity.

Evidence

Looking at how the simple picture compares to observational evidence.
